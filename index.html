CrewTrack

Smart RFID attendance and payroll tracker built for small work crews. No cloud, no subscription, no nonsense. Just tap a card and it works.

View on GitHub Learn More
Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
Built for real-world use in work vans and small offices. Rugged, simple, and reliable.
📡

RFID Tap-In

Workers tap their card on the reader. Attendance is logged instantly with date and time. Takes less than one second.

🛡️

Duplicate Detection

Same card tapped twice in a day? The device knows. It shows a warning instead of creating a duplicate entry.

📊

Monthly Reports

Automatic salary calculation. Days worked multiplied by daily wage. Know exactly who worked what, when.

📶

WiFi Dashboard

Connect your phone to the device's WiFi. Open a browser. Full dashboard to manage employees, view history, export data.

💾

SD Card Storage

All data stored locally on a MicroSD card. One CSV file per day. No internet required. Your data stays yours.

🔍

Search & Filter

Find any worker by name or card ID. Filter by month. See their full attendance history in seconds.

⏱️

Instant Boot

Powers on and is ready in under 2 seconds. No operating system to load. No updates to wait for. Just works.

🔋

Low Power

Runs on 50mA. Can be powered from a car cigarette lighter, power bank, or any USB adapter.

💰

Total Cost ~€15

ESP32 + RFID reader + LCD + SD card module. All cheap, all available on AliExpress. No proprietary hardware.

How It Works
Four steps. That's it.
From unboxing to tracking attendance in under 10 minutes.
1

Flash the Firmware

Connect ESP32 to your computer. Open Arduino IDE. Click Upload. Done.

2

Wire the Components

Connect RFID reader, LCD, SD card module, and buzzer to the ESP32. Follow the wiring diagram.

3

Register Workers

Connect your phone to the CrewTrack WiFi. Open the dashboard. Scan each worker's card and assign a name.

4

Use It Daily

Every morning, workers tap their cards. The device logs everything. Check reports from your phone anytime.

// What happens when a worker taps their card
if (employee found) {
  if (already scanned today) {
    show "DUPLICATE - George - Already Registered";
    beep(2 times);
  } else {
    write "E4913A21,07:34" → SD card;
    show "OK - George - Attendance Saved";
    beep(1 time);
  }
} else {
  show "??? - Unknown Card - Register via Dashboard";
  beep(long);
}
