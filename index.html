Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
Built for work vans and small offices. Simple, reliable, affordable.
📡

RFID Tap-In

Tap card, get confirmed. Attendance logged with timestamp in under 1 second.

🛡

Duplicate Detection

Same card twice today? Warning instead of duplicate entry. Data stays clean.

📈

Monthly Reports

Automatic salary calculation. Days worked times daily wage. No math needed.

📶

WiFi Dashboard

Connect phone to device WiFi. Full dashboard in browser. No app install.

💾

SD Card Storage

One CSV file per day. No internet needed. Your data stays on your card.

🔍

Search & Export

Find workers by name. Filter by month. Download CSV. Full history access.

How It Works
Four steps. Done.
1

Flash Firmware

Connect ESP32. Open Arduino IDE. Upload. 30 seconds.

2

Wire Components

Connect RFID, LCD, SD, buzzer. Follow the diagram.

3

Register Workers

Connect phone to WiFi. Open dashboard. Scan cards.

4

Use Daily

Workers tap cards each morning. Device handles the rest.
